Work

Brandify (Shopify clone)

Brandify is a Shopify clone project built with Flask and SQLAlchemy. It features a responsive and visually appealing e-commerce platform with user authentication, cart management, and order processing functionalities. The project optimizes performance and scalability through optimized database schemas using SQLAlchemy ORM. You can explore the source code on GitHub and experience the live version of Brandify.

Threadterest (Pinterest clone)

Threadterest was a group Pinterest clone project built with Flask and SQLAlchemy. The team utilized Git to effectively manage source code and facilitate seamless collaboration, enabling multiple developers to work on different features simultaneously. This project incorporates AWS S3 for efficient image storage and retrieval along with a personalized feed for the user.

JoinVibe (Meetup clone)

Inspired by the popular platform Meetup, this project aims to provide users with a similar experience of discovering and participating in events. The application incorporates several key features and technologies.

The project includes secure user authentication, RESTful APIs for data manipulation, advanced search and filtering, and a comprehensive event management system. With JoinVibe, users can easily find and join events based on their preferences, making event organization and participation seamless and enjoyable.

About

I am a value-driven individual, a team player, autodidact, and ambivert with a passion for travel. While pursuing my studies in Mechanical Engineering, I have also gained valuable experience running E-commerce brands, which has provided me with a well-rounded understanding of customer behavior, sales psychology, and innovative strategizing.

With a strong foundation in engineering, I am now eager to embark on a career where I can utilize my technical knowledge and combine it with my newfound skills to create products that have a meaningful impact on people's lives. Throughout my academic journey, I have discovered my innate ability to understand consumer psychology, which has been instrumental in designing products that resonate with their desires and expectations.

I am highly motivated by the prospect of building products that bring positive change to individuals, businesses, and personal growth journeys. My ultimate goal is to contribute to people's development and help them become the best versions of themselves. Whether through product creation, promotion, or branding, I see it as a valuable opportunity to exchange value and make a difference.

As I transition into the job market, I am excited to combine my past experiences with my technical skills to contribute to forward-thinking product development within the industry. I am eager to leverage my strong work ethic, collaborative nature, and passion for innovation to make a meaningful impact in my chosen field.
